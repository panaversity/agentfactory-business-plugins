ROLE REQUIREMENTS: Director-Level (General)
  Critical:
    - Strategic thinking and planning beyond functional boundaries
    - Cross-functional leadership and influence without direct authority
    - P&L or budget ownership at scale (typically £/$/€ multi-million)
    - Executive-level communication (board, C-suite, external stakeholders)
    - Building and developing high-performing teams (hiring, coaching, succession)
    - Decision-making under ambiguity with organisational-level consequences

  Important:
    - Change management and transformation leadership
    - External relationship management (clients, partners, regulators)
    - Commercial or revenue awareness across business units
    - Data-driven decision-making at strategic level

  Nice-to-have:
    - Industry thought leadership or external profile
    - M&A or integration experience
    - International/multi-market experience
