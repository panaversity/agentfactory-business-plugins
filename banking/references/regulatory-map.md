# Global Banking Regulatory Map

## Credit Loss Standards

| Jurisdiction                                 | Standard       | Body | Key Feature                         |
| -------------------------------------------- | -------------- | ---- | ----------------------------------- |
| IFRS jurisdictions (UK, EU, AU, SG, UAE, PK) | IFRS 9         | IASB | 3-stage ECL model, forward-looking  |
| USA                                          | CECL (ASC 326) | FASB | Lifetime ECL from day 1, no staging |
| Japan                                        | JGAAP          | ASBJ | Incurred loss + regulatory overlay  |

CRITICAL: IFRS 9 and CECL are fundamentally different. Never apply IFRS 9 staging to US banks.

## Basel III / IV Capital Implementation

| Jurisdiction | Framework                | Regulator    | Output Floor Status | Notes                              |
| ------------ | ------------------------ | ------------ | ------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| UK           | UK CRR (retained EU CRR) | PRA          | Consulting          | SME supporting factor retained     |
| EU           | CRR3                     | ECB/EBA      | 2025-2030 phase-in  | SME supporting factor removed      |
| USA          | Basel III Final Rule     | Fed/OCC/FDIC | Endgame proposed    | Higher leverage ratio (4%)         |
| Australia    | APS 110-120              | APRA         | Consulting          | "Unquestionably strong" 10.5% CET1 |
| Singapore    | MAS Notice 637           | MAS          | Consulting          | Higher minimums: CET1 6.5%, TC 10% |
| UAE          | CBUAE Standards          | CBUAE        | Consulting          | Higher minimums: CET1 7%, TC 10.5% |
| Pakistan     | SBP BPRD Circular        | SBP          | Adopting            | CET1 6%, TC 10%                    |

## AML / CFT Regulatory Map

| Jurisdiction | Primary Legislation        | FIU           | SAR/STR System   | Key Threshold                      |
| ------------ | -------------------------- | ------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| UK           | POCA 2002, MLRs 2017       | NCA           | SARs Online      | No amount threshold                |
| EU           | 6AMLD, AMLR (forthcoming)  | National FIUs | National systems | Varies by member state             |
| USA          | BSA 1970, PATRIOT Act      | FinCEN        | BSA E-Filing     | SAR: USD 5,000+; CTR: USD 10,000   |
| Australia    | AML/CTF Act 2006           | AUSTRAC       | AUSTRAC Online   | SMR: no threshold; TTR: AUD 10,000 |
| Singapore    | CDSA                       | STRO/CAD      | goAML            | No amount threshold                |
| UAE          | Federal Decree Law 20/2018 | UAE FIU       | goAML            | No amount threshold                |
| Pakistan     | AMLA 2010                  | FMU Pakistan  | goAML            | PKR 500,000 cash threshold         |

## FATF Status (check fatf-gafi.org for current listings)

| Category                              | Description                             | Implications                            |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| FATF Member                           | Full compliance with 40 Recommendations | Standard risk                           |
| FATF Grey List (Increased Monitoring) | Strategic deficiencies identified       | Enhanced scrutiny, EDD for customers    |
| FATF Black List (Call for Action)     | High-risk, non-cooperative              | Countermeasures, restrict relationships |

## Sanctions Regimes

| Authority           | Scope                           | Enforcement              | Extraterritorial          |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------- |
| OFAC (USA)          | Global (USD transactions)       | Civil + criminal         | Yes (secondary sanctions) |
| OFSI / HMT (UK)     | UK persons, entities, territory | Civil (strict liability) | Limited                   |
| EU Council (CFSP)   | EU persons, entities, territory | National enforcement     | Limited                   |
| UN Security Council | All UN member states            | Mandatory implementation | Via member states         |
