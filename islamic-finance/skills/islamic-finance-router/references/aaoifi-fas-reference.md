# AAOIFI Financial Accounting Standards — Quick Reference

This reference lists the AAOIFI Financial Accounting Standards (FAS) most relevant to the product skills in this repository. It is a practitioner's lookup table, not a comprehensive listing of all AAOIFI standards.

## Standards Referenced in This Repository

| FAS #            | Title                                                                                           | Applicable Products              | Key Provisions                                                                                                                 |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **FAS 2**        | Murabaha and Murabaha to the Purchase Orderer                                                   | Murabaha                         | Recognition at cost + profit margin; deferred profit as "Murabaha Income" not "Interest Income"; 4-step journal entry sequence |
| **FAS 4**        | Musharaka Financing                                                                             | Musharaka (Full), Musharaka (DM) | Partner's share recognised at cost; profit distribution per agreement; diminishing partner's share recognised progressively    |
| **FAS 7**        | Salam and Parallel Salam                                                                        | Salam                            | Full payment at inception; commodity as asset; parallel salam as separate obligation                                           |
| **FAS 10**       | Istisna'a and Parallel Istisna'a                                                                | Istisna'a                        | Progress-based recognition; parallel istisna'a as separate contract; contractor accounting                                     |
| **FAS 13**       | Disclosure of Bases for Profit Allocation between Owners' Equity and Investment Account Holders | All products with IAH            | Separation of owners' and IAH funds; profit allocation methodology disclosure                                                  |
| **FAS 25**       | Investment in Sukuk, Shares and Similar Instruments                                             | Sukuk (Investor)                 | Classification: amortised cost, fair value through equity, fair value through income statement                                 |
| **FAS 32**       | Ijarah and Ijarah Muntahia Bittamleek                                                           | Ijarah, IMB                      | Lessor retains asset on balance sheet; depreciation over useful life; IMB ownership transfer at end                            |
| **FAS 34**       | Financial Reporting for Sukuk-holders                                                           | Sukuk (Issuer)                   | Issuer derecognition tests; SPV consolidation; purchase undertaking treatment                                                  |
| **FAS 35**       | Risk Reserves                                                                                   | All products                     | Profit Equalisation Reserve (PER) and Investment Risk Reserve (IRR)                                                            |
| **Draft FAS 62** | Sukuk                                                                                           | Sukuk (Both)                     | Asset-based vs asset-backed distinction; most-watched proposal in Islamic finance standards                                    |

## Standards for Zakat

| Standard                | Title             | Key Provisions                                                                       |
| ----------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **FAS 9**               | Zakat             | Zakat base computation; P&L expense vs equity appropriation; disclosure requirements |
| **ZATCA Rules** (Saudi) | Zakat regulations | Equity-based formula; administratively determined; distinct from AAOIFI/Hanafi       |

## How to Use This Reference

1. **During product exercises**: Look up the applicable FAS number to verify the accounting treatment your agent produces
2. **When building SKILL.md files**: Reference the FAS number in the skill's "Applicable Standards" section
3. **For AAOIFI vs IFRS comparison**: Each FAS has an IFRS counterpart — FAS 2 vs IFRS 9 (murabaha), FAS 32 vs IFRS 16 (ijarah), FAS 25 vs IFRS 9 (sukuk investment)
4. **The key principle**: AAOIFI standards use Islamic finance terminology (Murabaha Income, not Interest Income) and may require different balance sheet presentation than IFRS
