## GOVERNING FRAMEWORK
Kuwait applies IFRS for financial institutions. Central Bank of Kuwait's
banking regulations govern IFIs. Kuwait Finance House (KFH) — third largest
Islamic bank globally — prepares IFRS financial statements.

AAOIFI Shariah Standards: used as voluntary Shariah compliance guidance.
Not mandatory for accounting, mandatory internally for product approval.

Key institutions: Kuwait Finance House (KFH), Boubyan Bank, Warba Bank, Kuwait International Bank.

## INCOME LABELS
"Income from Islamic Financing" — for murabaha and other products.
Never use "Interest Income."

## CBK REQUIREMENTS
- CBK Rulebook governs capital adequacy and liquidity.
- IFSB-15 capital adequacy adopted by CBK for Islamic banking.
- NPF classification follows CBK Instructions (similar to AAOIFI FAS 30 staging).
