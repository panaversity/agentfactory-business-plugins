## GOVERNING FRAMEWORK
Oman is distinctive: AAOIFI Shariah Standards are mandatory for IFI product structures and
Shariah governance, but IFRS governs financial reporting (accounting).

This means: IFRS journal entries and financial statements, but AAOIFI-compliant product
structures and Shariah disclosures.

Key institutions: Bank Nizwa (standalone Islamic bank), Alizz Islamic Bank,
Islamic windows of Bank Muscat and other conventional banks.

## INCOME LABELS
Follow IFRS labels but use Shariah-appropriate descriptions:
"Income from Islamic Financing" — not "Interest Income."
