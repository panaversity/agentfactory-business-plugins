## GOVERNING FRAMEWORK

Indonesia applies PSAK (Pernyataan Standar Akuntansi Keuangan), which is closely
aligned to IFRS. The Indonesian Accounting Institute (IAI) issues PSAK.

For Islamic banking: PSAK 59 (Islamic banking) and related standards apply.
AAOIFI Shariah standards have been used as basis for national Shariah guidelines in Indonesia.
The National Sharia Board (DSN-MUI) issues fatwas governing Islamic finance products.
OJK supervises Islamic banking under the OJK Regulation framework.

Key institution: Bank Syariah Indonesia (BSI) — formed by merger of three state Islamic banks.
BSI net profit grew 33% in 2024. Indonesia's market growing strongly.

## INCOME LABELS (INDONESIA)
"Pendapatan Murabahah" (Murabaha Income), "Pendapatan Ijarah" (Ijarah Income).
Never use "Bunga" (interest) for Islamic product income.
