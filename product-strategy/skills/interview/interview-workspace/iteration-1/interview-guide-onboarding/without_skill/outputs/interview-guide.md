# Interview Guide: Team Onboarding Process

## Overview
- **Goal**: Understand how mid-market companies handle onboarding
- **Target**: HR managers, team leads, people ops at mid-market companies
- **Duration**: 60 minutes

## Introduction (5 min)
Thank the participant. Explain the purpose of the interview. Get consent to record.

## Section 1: Background (10 min)
1. Tell me about your role and responsibilities.
2. How large is your organization?
3. How many people have you onboarded in the last year?

## Section 2: Current Onboarding Process (15 min)
4. Can you describe your current onboarding process?
5. What tools or systems do you use for onboarding?
6. How long does your typical onboarding take?
7. Who is involved in the onboarding process?
8. What documentation do new hires receive?

## Section 3: Pain Points and Challenges (15 min)
9. What are the biggest challenges you face with onboarding?
10. Have you ever had an onboarding experience go poorly? What happened?
11. What do new hires complain about most during onboarding?
12. How do you measure onboarding success?
13. What would an ideal onboarding experience look like for you?

## Section 4: Feature Exploration (10 min)
14. We're considering building a team onboarding feature. What would be most valuable to you?
15. Would you find it helpful to have automated task assignments for new hires?
16. How important is it to have a centralized onboarding dashboard?
17. Would you pay for a tool that streamlines onboarding?

## Section 5: Wrap-Up (5 min)
18. Is there anything else about onboarding you'd like to share?
19. Can we follow up with you if we have additional questions?

## Notes
- Record responses carefully
- Note body language and enthusiasm levels
- Look for patterns across interviews
