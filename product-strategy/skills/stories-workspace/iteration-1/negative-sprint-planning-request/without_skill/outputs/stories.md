# Not a Story Refinement Request

This request is about **sprint planning** (selecting stories from backlog to fill a velocity target), not about writing or refining user stories.

## What You Asked For

- Sprint planning with a 42-point velocity
- Pulling stories from a backlog

## Why This Isn't My Job

Story writing/refinement focuses on crafting well-structured user stories with acceptance criteria, edge cases, and clear scope. Sprint planning is a separate discipline that involves:

- Backlog prioritization against sprint goals
- Capacity allocation across team members
- Dependency sequencing
- Commitment negotiation

## Recommendation

Use **/sprint-planning** for sprint-level planning. That tool handles velocity-based story selection, capacity modeling, and sprint goal alignment.

If you need help **writing or refining individual stories** before they go into sprint planning, come back here with the story details.
