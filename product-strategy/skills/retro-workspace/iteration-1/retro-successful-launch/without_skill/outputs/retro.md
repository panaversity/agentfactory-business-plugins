**Feature**: New Onboarding Flow

- **Clean delivery**: On time, no scope changes, all acceptance criteria met.
- **Team morale**: The team felt great about this one, which is always a positive signal.

## What Didn't Go Well

- Nothing major to flag. This was a well-executed feature.
