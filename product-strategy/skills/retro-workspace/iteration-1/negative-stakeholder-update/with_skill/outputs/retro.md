## Not a Retrospective — Redirecting

You're asking for a **stakeholder update**, not a product retrospective. These are
different workflows with different audiences and outputs:

- **Retrospective**: Internal team exercise that evaluates whether a shipped feature
  solved its problem, assesses delivery quality, and produces specific process improvements.
  Audience: the team that built it.

- **Stakeholder update**: Executive-facing status communication that summarizes progress,
  highlights risks, and requests decisions. Audience: leadership.

**For a stakeholder update**, use `/stakeholder-update` — it's designed for executive
communication with the right framing, level of detail, and format.

If you actually do want to run a retrospective on the search feature (to evaluate whether
it solved the problem it was designed to solve), I can help with that — but I'll need
outcome data (adoption, support ticket trends, success metrics) from at least 4-12 weeks
post-launch.
