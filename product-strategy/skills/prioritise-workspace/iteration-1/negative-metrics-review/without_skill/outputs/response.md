# Q2 Product Metrics Review

I'd be happy to help review your product metrics! However, I'll need access to your actual data to provide meaningful analysis. Here's a framework for what to look at:

## Key Metrics to Review

### MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Current MRR vs. previous quarter
- MRR growth rate (month-over-month)
- Breakdown: new MRR, expansion MRR, churned MRR, contraction MRR
- Net MRR retention rate

### Churn Rate
- Customer churn rate (logos lost / total customers)
- Revenue churn rate (MRR lost / total MRR)
- Gross vs. net churn (does expansion offset losses?)
- Churn by cohort — are newer customers churning faster?

### NPS Trend
- Current NPS score
- Trend over last 4 quarters
- Breakdown by customer segment
- Key drivers of detractor scores

## What I'd Need From You

To do a proper review, could you share:
1. Your metrics dashboard or data export
2. Any specific concerns or hypotheses you want to test
3. Context on recent product changes that might affect metrics

Would you like to share your data so I can dig in?
