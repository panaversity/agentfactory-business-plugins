## Approach

1. **Sort your backlog by priority** — What's most important to the business right now?
2. **Pull from the top** — Add stories until you approach 40 points
3. **Check dependencies** — Make sure nothing is blocked
4. **Verify skill availability** — Do you have the right people for the stories you're pulling in?

- Don't fill to exactly 42 points — leave breathing room
