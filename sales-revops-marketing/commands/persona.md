# /persona

Buyer persona and ICP definition or refinement.

## Usage

/persona [market-or-context]

## Examples

/persona "Mid-market logistics companies in the UK, 200-2000 employees"
/persona "Fintech CTOs evaluating compliance automation, Series B+"
/persona "Refine existing ICP — we are closing better in healthcare than manufacturing"

## Workflow

1. Route to persona-icp skill via sales-marketing-global-router
2. Gather market context: industry, geography, company size, maturity
3. Define firmographic criteria: revenue, headcount, industry, geography, tech stack
4. Identify decision makers, influencers, and blockers in the buying committee
5. Map pain points and buying triggers to your solution
6. Create persona profile with messaging themes and channel preferences

## Output

- Mandatory header block (task, market context, configuration, verify data)
- ICP definition: firmographic and technographic criteria with ranges
- Buyer persona(s): role, demographics, motivations, day-in-the-life
- Objections and how to address them
- Pain points mapped to your solution capabilities
- Buying triggers and timing signals to watch for
- Preferred channels and content formats per persona
- Messaging themes and positioning angles

VALIDATE PERSONA ASSUMPTIONS AGAINST YOUR ACTUAL CUSTOMER BASE
