# /copy

Ad copy, landing pages, subject lines, CTAs, and A/B variants.

## Usage

/copy [type] [context]

## Examples

/copy "email subject lines" "Webinar invite for VP Ops in logistics, topic: warehouse automation ROI"
/copy "LinkedIn ad" "Targeting CTOs in fintech, promoting free assessment tool"
/copy "landing page" "Product launch, mid-market SaaS buyers, emphasis on time-to-value"

## Workflow

1. Route to copywriting skill via sales-marketing-global-router
2. Identify copy type: ad, landing page, subject line, CTA, tagline, or body copy
3. Load brand voice guidelines and ICP from sales-marketing.local.md if configured
4. Generate 2-3 copy variants with distinct angles or hooks
5. Apply Five Laws compliance check where the copy involves outreach
6. Provide A/B testing recommendations and platform-specific formatting notes

## Output

- Mandatory header block (task, copy type, audience, configuration, verify data)
- Copy variants (2-3) with distinct positioning angles
- Character and word counts per variant
- Tone analysis: formality, urgency, emotional appeal
- Five Laws compliance check (for outreach-related copy)
- A/B testing notes: what each variant tests and how to measure
- Platform-specific formatting guidance where applicable
