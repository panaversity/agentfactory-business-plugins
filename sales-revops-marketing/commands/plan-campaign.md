# /plan-campaign

Full campaign brief with strategy, channel mix, budget allocation, and success metrics.

## Usage

/plan-campaign goal:[goal] audience:[persona] budget:[amount] timeline:[period]

## Examples

/plan-campaign goal:"lead gen" audience:"VP Ops, mid-market logistics" budget:"GBP 25,000" timeline:"Q3 2026"
/plan-campaign goal:"product launch" audience:"CTOs, fintech" budget:"USD 50,000" timeline:"6 weeks"
/plan-campaign goal:"ABM" audience:"named accounts list" budget:"GBP 15,000" timeline:"Q4"

## Workflow

1. Route to campaign-planning skill via sales-marketing-global-router
2. Load ICP, personas, and brand voice from sales-marketing.local.md if configured
3. Collect brief: goal, audience, offer, budget, timeline, constraints
4. Select primary strategy: thought leadership / direct response / ABM / product-led
5. Design channel mix with budget allocation per channel
6. Define lead magnet and sales-marketing integration flow
7. Create content calendar summary
8. Set success metrics with targets and decision gates
9. Identify risks and contingency plans

## Output

- Campaign brief with strategy rationale
- Audience definition (primary, secondary, estimated size)
- Channel mix with budget allocation per phase
- Lead magnet and conversion flow
- Sales-marketing integration (enrichment, scoring, routing, sequence)
- Content calendar summary
- Success metrics: primary KPI, secondary KPIs, cost targets
- Risk and contingency plan
- Budget summary
