## CALENDAR GENERATION WORKFLOW

### Input Required

1. Campaign or period being planned (e.g. Q2 campaign / 12 weeks)
2. Campaign brief (load from /campaign output or paste)
3. Channels to cover (LinkedIn / Email / Blog / Social / Paid / Webinar)
4. Content production resources (who, how many pieces per week)
5. Key dates: product launches, events, seasonal peaks, deadlines
6. Pillar content (cornerstone asset the calendar is built around, if any)

### Calendar Architecture Principles

THE PILLAR-CLUSTER MODEL:
Pillar content: 1 major asset per 4-6 weeks (whitepaper, guide, report)
Cluster content: 4-8 derived pieces per pillar (posts, emails, snippets)
All cluster content links back to the pillar.
This multiplies reach from each research investment.

CHANNEL CADENCE (sustainable defaults):
LinkedIn company page: 3x/week
CEO/VP personal LinkedIn: 4-5x/week (ghostwritten)
Email newsletter: 1-2x/month (do not email for the sake of it)
Blog: 1-2x/week (SEO compound value)
Webinar: 1x/month (mid-funnel; high intent)
Paid social: Continuous (sync with organic calendar)

### Calendar Output Format

Produce as a table:

| Week | Date   | Channel            | Content Title / Description | Format     | Persona   | CTA   | Status |
| ---- | ------ | ------------------ | --------------------------- | ---------- | --------- | ----- | ------ |
| 1    | [Date] | LinkedIn (company) | [Title]                     | [Format]   | [Persona] | [CTA] | Draft  |
| 1    | [Date] | Email              | [Title]                     | Newsletter | [Persona] | [CTA] | Draft  |
| 2    | [Date] | Blog               | [Title]                     | Long-form  | [Persona] | [CTA] | Brief  |

Then produce for each week:
KEY THEME: [The connecting thread across all content that week]
PILLAR LINK: [Which cornerstone asset this week's content supports]
PRODUCTION DEADLINE: [When content must be ready to publish on schedule]

### Content Workload Calculator

Sustainable weekly output by team size:
1 writer (full-time): 3-4 pieces/week (mix of short and long)
1 writer (part-time): 1-2 pieces/week

- AI-assisted: 2-3x multiplier on same headcount
  Editorial review: Allow 20% of creation time for review

IF production plan exceeds team capacity: flag and recommend what to cut.
Prioritise: pillar content > high-converting formats > new channels.

## NEVER DO THESE

- NEVER plan more content than the team can produce at quality --
  a gap in the calendar is better than filler content
- NEVER publish without a CTA -- every piece should have a next step
- NEVER plan content that is not anchored to a persona and a campaign goal
- NEVER build a calendar without accounting for review and approval time --
  plan production deadlines 5 business days before publish dates
- NEVER plan for a channel you are not committed to operating consistently --
  an abandoned LinkedIn page or newsletter does more damage than none
