## ICP AND PERSONA DEVELOPMENT WORKFLOW

### Step 1: ICP Discovery Interview

Conduct this interview with sales leadership and top-performing reps:

Firmographic questions:

1. What company size (employees + revenue) have your best customers been?
2. Which industries buy fastest and expand most?
3. What geographies convert at the highest rate?
4. Is there a technology maturity pattern in your best customers?

Timing signal questions: 5. What was happening at your best customers' companies when they first reached out or responded? 6. What event or trigger most often precedes a buying decision? 7. Are there times of year when your best customers typically buy?

Anti-ICP questions (equally important): 8. What company type engages heavily but never buys? (time-waster profile) 9. What company type buys but churns quickly? (wrong-fit profile) 10. What company type is resistant to your product regardless of fit?

### Step 2: Persona Interview

For each buying persona (target contact, economic buyer, champion):

Role and motivation:

- What is their primary professional goal this year?
- What does success look like in their role?
- What would make them look good to their leadership?

Pain and problem:

- What is their biggest operational or strategic frustration?
- What do they spend too much time on?
- What keeps them up at night (professionally)?

Buying behaviour:

- How do they discover new tools and vendors?
- Who do they trust for recommendations?
- What would make them choose not to buy at all?
- What objections do they most commonly raise?

Information consumption:

- What do they read? (trade press, LinkedIn, newsletters, podcasts)
- What events do they attend?
- Who are the industry voices they follow?

Role: [Title range]

WHO THEY ARE

Primary goal: [Professional outcome they are measured on]

Primary: [LinkedIn / Email / Phone / Events / Trade press]
Content: [What they read and follow]
Timing: [When they are most receptive]
================================================================

## NEVER DO THESE

- NEVER build an ICP from assumptions alone -- always validate against
  actual closed-won customer data and conversations with sales leadership
- NEVER skip the anti-ICP analysis -- knowing who NOT to pursue saves
  more sales time than knowing who to pursue
- NEVER produce an ICP or persona without actionable outreach guidance --
  a persona that cannot be translated into specific messaging and channel
  selection is an academic exercise, not a sales tool
- NEVER treat the ICP as static -- recalibrate quarterly against actual
  conversion data and new market signals
