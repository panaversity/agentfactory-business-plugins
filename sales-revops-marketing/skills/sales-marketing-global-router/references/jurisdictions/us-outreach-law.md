## GOVERNING FRAMEWORK

This overlay provides awareness for automated systems generating outreach
content targeting US-based prospects. This is NOT legal advice. Consult
qualified legal counsel for compliance requirements.

### CAN-SPAM Act 2003 (15 U.S.C. 7701-7713)

Governs ALL commercial email messages sent to US recipients.

Key requirements for outreach systems:

- Subject lines must not be deceptive or misleading
- Message must be identified as an advertisement or solicitation
  (unless prior business relationship exists)
- Physical postal address of sender MUST be included in every email
- Opt-out mechanism must be present, clear, and conspicuous
- Opt-out requests must be honoured within 10 business days
- Once opted out: NEVER send commercial email to that address again
- Cannot sell or transfer opted-out email addresses

Penalties: up to USD 51,744 per violation (adjusted annually for inflation)

ESCALATION TRIGGER: Any bulk email campaign to US recipients -> verify
CAN-SPAM compliance before sending: postal address, opt-out link, honest
subject line, sender identification

### FTC Telemarketing Sales Rule (TSR)

Governs telephone-based outreach and cold calling.

Key requirements:

- National Do Not Call Registry: check before any cold call
- Company-specific Do Not Call list: maintain and honour
- Calling hours: 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM recipient's local time only
- Caller ID: must transmit accurate caller ID information
- Robocalls: prior express written consent required for prerecorded messages
- Cannot call from blocked or spoofed numbers

ESCALATION TRIGGER: Any phone-based outreach campaign -> verify DNC
registry compliance and calling hour restrictions

### Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)

Governs calls, texts, and faxes.

Key requirements:

- Prior express consent required for autodialed calls to mobile phones
- Prior express written consent required for telemarketing via autodialer
  or prerecorded voice to mobile phones
- SMS/text messages: treated as calls under TCPA; consent required
- Private right of action: USD 500 per violation; USD 1,500 if willful

ESCALATION TRIGGER: Any SMS or automated calling campaign to US mobile
numbers -> obtain documented consent before sending

### State-Specific Outreach Regulations

California (CCPA/CPRA):

- B2B contact data is covered by CCPA as of January 2023
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt out of sale/sharing of personal information
- Data enrichment from third parties: verify data broker compliance
- Penalties: up to USD 7,500 per intentional violation

ESCALATION TRIGGER: Any data enrichment or prospect research on
California-based contacts -> verify CCPA compliance of data sources

New York:

- NY telemarketing registration required for businesses making
  outreach calls to NY consumers
- Additional DNC requirements beyond federal

Illinois (BIPA):

- If any biometric data is used in prospect identification or
  verification -> immediate escalation (private right of action)

### LinkedIn and Social Media Outreach

LinkedIn Terms of Service:

- Automated messaging prohibited without LinkedIn API access
- Connection request limits: typically 100-200 per week
- InMail limits: based on subscription tier
- Scraping LinkedIn data: prohibited under ToS and potentially
  under Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)

ESCALATION TRIGGER: Any plan to automate LinkedIn outreach at scale
-> verify compliance with LinkedIn ToS and CFAA

### Data Enrichment and Third-Party Data

FTC Act Section 5 (unfair or deceptive practices):

- Third-party data must be obtained through legitimate means
- Enrichment providers must comply with applicable privacy laws
- Cannot use data obtained through deceptive practices

Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA):

- Background check data cannot be used for marketing without
  permissible purpose
- Credit data: never use for outreach targeting

## KEY US-SPECIFIC OUTREACH TRIGGERS

- CAN-SPAM: every commercial email to US recipients requires postal
  address, opt-out link, honest subject line
- TCPA: SMS and automated calls to mobile phones require prior
  express written consent
- CCPA: B2B data covered; prospect research must use compliant sources
- DNC Registry: check before every cold calling campaign
- BIPA: any biometric data in prospect identification -> immediate escalation
- LinkedIn: no automated messaging without API access; respect connection limits
- Calling hours: 8 AM - 9 PM recipient local time only
