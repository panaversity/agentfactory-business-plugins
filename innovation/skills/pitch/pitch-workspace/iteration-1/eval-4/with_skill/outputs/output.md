## Data I Need

### For Unit Economics (CAC, LTV, Payback)

| Data Point | What to provide |
|---|---|
| **Total sales & marketing spend** | Last 3-6 months, monthly |
| **New customers acquired** | Same period, monthly |
| **Average revenue per customer** | Monthly or annual |
| **Gross margin %** | After COGS/delivery costs |
| **Monthly churn rate** | % of customers lost per month (or cohort retention data) |
| **Sales cycle length** | Average days from first touch to close |

### For 3-Scenario Financial Model

| Data Point | What to provide |
|---|---|
| **Current MRR/ARR** | As of now |
| **Monthly burn rate** | Total monthly spend |
| **Cash in bank** | Current balance |
| **Raise amount & timing** | What you're raising, when you expect to close |
| **Team plan** | Current headcount + planned hires |
| **Growth rate** | Current month-over-month revenue growth % |
| **Key cost categories** | Engineering, sales, ops, infra — rough split |

## What I'll Build Once You Provide This

1. **CAC** — total acquisition cost per customer, broken down by channel if possible
2. **LTV** — using gross-margin-adjusted revenue × average customer lifetime (1/churn)
3. **LTV:CAC ratio** — with honest assessment of evidence quality
4. **Payback period** — months to recover CAC from gross profit
5. **3-scenario model** (Base / Conservative / Aggressive) with:
   - Monthly P&L projection (18 months)
   - Cash runway under each scenario
   - Key inflection points and milestones
   - Assumptions clearly flagged as validated vs. assumed
