## Questions I need answered before I can size this market

### 1. Customer Definition
- **Who exactly buys this?** (e.g., mid-market companies with 50-500 employees? Enterprise? SMBs? Specific verticals like construction, healthcare, manufacturing?)
- **What's the job title of the buyer?** (CFO? Controller? AP Manager? Head of Finance?)
- **What's the minimum company profile** where your tool makes sense? (e.g., "companies processing at least 500 invoices/month")

### 2. Pricing & Unit Economics
- **How do you charge?** (per invoice? per user? flat SaaS subscription? tiered?)
- **What's your current or planned ACV** (annual contract value)?
- **Do you have any paying customers or pilots yet?** If so, what are they paying?

### 3. Geographic Scope
- **Where are you selling?** (US only? North America? Global? Specific country?)

### 4. What You Replace
- **What's the current alternative?** (manual AP in QuickBooks? Bill.com? SAP? Spreadsheets + email?)
- **What's your specific differentiator** vs. existing AP automation (Tipalti, Bill.com, Stampli, Coupa, MineralTree)?

### 5. Go-to-Market Entry Point
- **Which segment are you selling to first?** This determines your SOM.
- **What's your current distribution channel?** (direct sales? partnerships with accountants? PLG?)

- **TAM**: Total addressable based on countable companies × your ACV
- **SAM**: Filtered by your actual reachable segment (geo, size, vertical)

No "$X trillion market opportunity" nonsense — just math an investor can check.
